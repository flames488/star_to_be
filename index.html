Our Story

Built in Ibeju-Lekki.
Destined for Greatness.

Star To-Be Soccer Academy was born in 2023 with one mission: to unlock the extraordinary potential of young footballers in Ibeju-Lekki, Lagos. We don't just train players — we build complete athletes, leaders, and champions.

From our first match at Elesekan Field to memorable visits at the Liberty Stadium and victories in the Masters League, every step has been a testament to the relentless spirit of the STB family.

The Next Generation

Our junior programme is the heart of Star To-Be. We nurture raw talent from as young as 8 years old, building technique, confidence, and the mental fortitude to compete at the highest levels.

From Ibeju-Lekki to the Liberty Stadium — every junior player at STB carries the same dream: to become unstoppable. We give them the coaching, the structure, and the belief to get there.

Structured Age-Group Coaching from U8 to U17
Fitness & Sports Science tailored for youth development
Weekly league fixtures & inter-academy tournaments
Mentorship by senior STB first-team players
Clear pathway to the first-team squad
